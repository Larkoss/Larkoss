Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Bettr is a social sports-prediction app for entertainment only. No real money is ever staked, won, or lost on this platform.

1. Who we are

Bettr is a social sports-prediction platform where users share picks, follow other bettors, and track prediction records — purely for fun. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data — email address, username, and password (stored as a one-way bcrypt hash; we never store your plain-text password).
  • Profile data — optional bio and profile photo you choose to upload.
  • Activity data — predictions (bets) you create and post publicly to the feed, comments, likes, and follow relationships.
  • Google account data — if you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and a unique Google identifier to link your account. We do not receive your Google password or contacts.
  • Technical data — IP addresses and standard HTTP request headers, retained in server logs for up to 30 days for security and debugging.

We do not collect location data, device contacts, payment information, or biometric data.

3. How we use your data

  • To create and authenticate your account.
  • To display your public profile and predictions to other users.
  • To send transactional emails — specifically, password-reset links when you request one. We do not send marketing emails.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, spam, or fraud.
  • To operate and improve the service.

4. Third-party services

Google Sign-In (optional) — governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We use it solely to verify your identity and will never request access beyond your basic profile.

We do not integrate advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may disclose information only when required to do so by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your email address, username, password hash, Google identifier, profile bio, and profile photo are permanently removed from our systems.

Predictions (bets) you created may remain in the database in anonymised form (with no link to your email or username) to preserve the integrity of historical leaderboard data.

7. Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Access your data by viewing your profile in the app.
  • Correct your data by editing your username, bio, or profile photo.
  • Delete your account — and all associated personal data — from within the app: Settings → Edit Profile → Delete Account. Deletion takes effect immediately.
  • Request a password reset at any time from the login screen.

For any other data requests, contact us at the address below.

8. Age requirement

You must confirm you meet the minimum age required in your jurisdiction to use prediction and betting-related content. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below that age. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of Bettr after a revision constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
